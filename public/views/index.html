Welcome to The Lodge

Here at The Lodge, we provide fine dining, two different lodging types, and ample opportunities to explore the mountain life for which the community is known.

Eat breakfast, lunch, dinner, or enjoy a hand-crafted cocktail at our world-class restaurant.

Sleep in the main lodge or one of our A-frame cottages with views of the Setetchwa Mountains.

Play in the great outdoors. Hike existing trails (or blaze your own), fish, swim, and enjoy an outdoor campfire.
